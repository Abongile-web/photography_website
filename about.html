Abongile William, otherwise known as kapturedbyviibez, is a 21 year old ICT Multimedia student who recently found his love for photography and capturing beautiful moments of people.

His Story..

5 years ago towards the end of his high school career, Abongile knew he's always wanted to be a creative. His first love was his love for sound. Right after finishing matric he attended SAE to do, and later complete, a Higher Certificate in Sound Production to polish up his skills as a studio sound engineer and further assist artist with the quality of their sound, but it did not end there..

In 2018 he joined CPUT to start his qualification in ICT and during the course he started gravitating towards Multimedia, specifically design, photography and front-end developement. With the skills he has gathered over the course and his habit of being productive, he advances to pursue a career in everything Multimedia and enjoy every step of the way while doing so!
